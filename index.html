Problem: Education is expensive, and will continue being expensive due to their inflated value and their promise of giving students a miraculous windfall once they graduate.

Solution: Provide a cheap, accessible way for people to get a solid education through live (and non-live) lectures and educational materials. We'll have people who like to teach set up live lectures and educational materials, and students will be able to watch the lectures and ask them questions.

Here's how it would work:
1. You sign up for a class.
2. You pay a small enrollment fee up front.
3. You take the class.
4. If at the end of the learning period, you feel like you got something out of the class, you can let your "professor" keep the money. If you did not enjoy the class, you can get a full refund and basically have taken the class for free.

FAQ

Are there any credentials for people who will teach on the website?
No, but there will be mechanisms to prevent unqualified people to continue teaching on the site: being able to get a full refund no questions asked is one of them; reviews for the teachers will be another.
Why are there no qualifications for people who can teach on this website you want to build?
I want to be able to provide an open and accessible way for people to teach so that the people who want to teach, can. The people who can teach, stay. Often times, university professors just want to get tenure and do a bad job at actually educating people, while people who teach on here will teach due to their desire to be pedagogical. People here will essentially be able to teach without climbing the academia ladder.
How much will each course cost?
The goal will be for each course to cost less than a textbook per person. Though it is theoretically free if you get a full refund at the end.
What's to prevent me from taking a bunch of classes and never paying for a single one by getting a refund every time?
Nothing; however, the hope is that you do not, especially if you feel like whatever you learned was educational and has given you a skill that you can use later on in life.
